Ingredients
	2 cups organic kale
	1 cup coconut milk
	2 Tbsp unsalted organic grass fed butter
	1 Cucumber
	1 tsp cinnamon
	2 Tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
	2 Tbsp MCT oil
	500mg calcium
	1/4 tsp Himalayan Sea salt
	2 Tbsp gelatin


Instructions
	Put all ingredients except gelatin into blender.
	Blend well until there are no more kale chunks.
	Mix in gelatin at low speeed or with spoon
